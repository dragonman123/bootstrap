Default format of how to use tables.

<div class="container">
  <table class="table">
    <thead>
      <tr>
        <th>Firstname</th>
        <th>Lastname</th>
        <th>Email</th>
      </tr>
    </thead>
    <tbody>
      <tr>
        <td>John</td>
        <td>Doe</td>
        <td>john@example.com</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td>Mary</td>
        <td>Moe</td>
        <td>mary@example.com</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td>July</td>
        <td>Dooley</td>
        <td>july@example.com</td>
      </tr>
    </tbody>
  </table>
</div>


Here, 
thead is for heading for the table.
tbody contains the content of the table.
Now coming to properties of the table.
class->
.table, basic bootstrap table class..
.table-striped, striped table, show some background color in the backgound.
.table-bordered, show the border of the table columns and rows.
.table-hover, highlight table's row when you hover mouse over it.
.table-condensed, a little less padding for our table.
